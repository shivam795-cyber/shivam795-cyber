# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails  
- Always verify the sender before sharing information  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication on important accounts  
- Keep your software and apps updated  
- Never share OTP or banking details with anyone  
- Always check UPI payment requests before approving  
- Use trusted and secure websites for transactions
